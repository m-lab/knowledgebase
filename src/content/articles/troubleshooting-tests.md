Most M-Lab tests complete in under thirty seconds. If a test is taking longer than that, or fails to start or complete, work through the steps below before reporting a bug.

## Initial Steps

1. **Run the test again.** An unusual network issue may have caused a one-off failure.
2. **Try a different location.** If the test fails at home, does it work at a coffee shop or library? This helps isolate whether the problem is specific to your network.
3. **Check for a firewall.** If the test never starts or never completes, your connection may be behind a firewall. This is common on managed workplace networks that restrict certain types of traffic.

## Reporting a Bug

If none of the above resolves the issue, email [support@measurementlab.net](mailto:support@measurementlab.net). To help M-Lab support diagnose the problem, include as much of the following as possible:

- Your operating system
- Your browser name and version
- The name of the test that is not working
- Any error messages you received

M-Lab support typically replies within five business days.

You can also ask other M-Lab users for help in the [M-Lab-Discuss group](https://groups.google.com/a/measurementlab.net/g/discuss).
